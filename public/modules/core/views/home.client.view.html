Welcome to Cognition

Cognition is a web application that enhences the classroom environment for learning with interactive questions created by the professor. These questions are bundled into a quiz that can be broadcasted to show up on each of the students' device when the professor chooses to display them. It has various features such as grading/releasing the quiz as feedback for the students who took it, real-time update on how many students answered the aired question (as well as their choice), saved released quiz for future access, and much more.
